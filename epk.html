Artist Bio

Die Gryn is a queer pop artist, singer and songwriter whose sound blends danceable pop energy with emotional clarity and self-assured lyricism. Drawing from alt-pop, disco-influenced grooves and modern club sensibilities, his music balances movement with meaning. His debut single Fake Love (released 21 November 2025) introduces Gryn’s ability to transform complicated romantic experiences into a statement of self-worth and personal empowerment. The track explores boundaries, emotional honesty, and the refusal to accept mistreatment, all while remaining rhythm-driven and immediately engaging. Following the original release, Fake Love has continued to evolve through collaborative reinterpretations, including an official remix that expands the track’s sonic reach and audience. With a strong visual identity and a growing presence across streaming and social platforms, Die Gryn is building momentum toward a broader body of work set to unfold through 2026.
